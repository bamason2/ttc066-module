# Volumes: Intake Manifold

$$ \frac{dm}{dt}=\dot{m}_{in}-\dot{m}_{out} \label{eq1} $$

Energy balance;

$$ \frac{dE}{dt}=\dot{m}_{in}h_{0}_{in}-\dot{m}_{out}h_{0}_{out}+\dot{Q} \label{eq2} $$

where;

$$ h_0=C_pT+\frac{u^2}{2} $$

$$ E = mc_vT+\frac{mu^2}{2}+mgz $$

- GPE change is 0
- KE change is 0
